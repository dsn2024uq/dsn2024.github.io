Accommodation

Renaissance Harborplace Hotel

The conference has reserved a limited number of rooms at the conference hotel, Renaissance Harborplace Hotel. Note that Baltimore might be very busy in the summer time, please make your reservation ASAP. The booking link is: https://book.passkey.com/go/IEEEDSN

Please follow these steps: Click Make Reservation -> Select dates (1st date is the check-in date and 2nd date is the check-out date) and number of rooms and guests -> Click Search ->Select Room -> Provide all requested information -> Payment

The conference negotiated rate for a standard room is $129 per night, breakfast not included. Note that attendees may get the hotel breakfast for an additional $25 per person per day. You may get coupons from the hotel at check-in and then use them to present to get the discounted rate in the restaurant.

NOTE: The hotel cutoff date is May 25, 2022. After that date, you will not be able to get the conference rate.
