Registration

Authors should read the policy for accepted papers.

You can register for DSN 2022 here: https://cvent.me/2ExV4r

If a letter supporting a US entrance visa is needed, please check the appropriate box during registration.

Please email registration+DSN@computer.org if you need any registration assistance. For questions regarding author submissions, please contact Saman Zonouz at saman.zonouz@rutgers.edu.

The conference registration fees are:

  • Main Conference Authors or Workshop Authors attending full conference
    • IEEE / IFIP / Student / Life member: $850
    • Non-member / Student non-member: $1020
  • Full conference:
    • IEEE/IFIP member: $850
    • IEEE Student member: $595
    • IEEE Life member: $470
    • Non-member: $1020
    • Student non-member: $715
  • Workshop authors attending workshops only
    • IEEE / IFIP / Student / Life member: $400
    • Non-member / Student non-member: $480
  • Workshops only:
    • IEEE/IFIP member: $400
    • IEEE Student member: $280
    • IEEE Life member: $280
    • Non-member: $480
    • Student non-member: $340

Each main conference paper must have a full conference author registration. Each workshop paper must have either a workshop or a full conference author registration.

Cancellation policy:
All refund/cancellation requests must be received in writing to registration+DSN@computer.org by June 13, 2022. There will be an administrative fee of $100 USD ($50 USD for workshops only registrations) deducted from each refund. You can modify your registration using the "Already Registered?" link on the registration site.

Policy for Accepted Papers

  1. Every paper has to have a full registration to appear in the conference proceedings.
  2. The conference is in person and papers are presented only in person.
  3. If none of the authors can come to the conference specifically because none of them could get a US entrance visa, then the talk can be delegated to another person that is familiar with the work and can present it in person at the conference. Otherwise, the work will not be presented in the conference but will appear in the proceedings.
  4. We invite the authors of all the papers to submit (if they want) a one-minute teaser video that will be posted on the conference program web page before the conference. We similarly invite a 20 minute video presenting the paper that will be posted on the conference program web page after the conference. Instructions for video submissions will be provided to the authors by email.
