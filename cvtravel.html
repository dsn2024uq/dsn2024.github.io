Directions from BWI to Renaissance Harborplace Hotel
  • Uber and Lyft.
    You need the Uber or Lyft app on your phone.
    The price is also around 25--40 USD depending on traffic and availability.
  • By taxi, it takes 20 minutes, depending on the traffic. The cost is around 25--40 USD.
  • Shuttle services are also available. Please see this URL for details.
  • Public transportation.
    Light RailLink - HUNT VALLEY & CROMWELL is available, which runs every 30 mins. Please refer to this URL for more details.

    (2) Get off the tram at the "Baltimore Arena" station.
    (3) After getting off, walk half a block south to "W Lombard St."
    Then, walk about six blocks east to "South St." and turn right (facing south).
